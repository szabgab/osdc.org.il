=title Open Source Developers Club
=name Open Source Developers Club - Modiin branch

We provide learning opportunities and mentoring on your way to start your career in the hi-tech world
or improve your employability, if you are already in the field.

We belive the being involved in Open Source development is good for your employment opportunities.

Some of the subjects you will use in our projects. If you don't know any of these or if they don't mean anything to you don't worry.
We'll explain everything.

<ul>
   <li>Git and GitHub for Version Control</li>
   <li>Travis-CI for Continuous Integration</li>
   <li>JSON</li>
   <li>Python</li>
   <li>Pytest and testing Python in general</li>
   <li>Docker</li>
</ul>

<h2>Modiin branch</h2>

<img class="logo" src="/img/yuda.jpg" />

The OSDC Modiin has weekly meeting in Yuda, the Youth Center of Modiin.

Visit <a href="https://www.meetup.com/Modiin-Tech-Talks">our Meetup page</a> where you'll find the date and time of our next meeting.
