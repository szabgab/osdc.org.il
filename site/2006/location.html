Location

For a printable map click here

Transportation

Train

The Netanya Academic College is only a short taxi ride from the Netanya train station.

Car

There is a huge parking slot in front of the campus that costs 10 NIS/day. In a few hundred meters distance there is plenty of free parking.

Coming from Tel Aviv (from the south) on the main sea-shore road pass the Poleg interchange (the one with IKEA) and on the next interchange turn right in the direction of Kfar Netter. From there on you will have clear signs to the Netanya Academic Collage.
Basically you go straight to east till a roundabout, where Kfar Netter is the righ (first) turn and NAC is the left (second) turn. Then go more or less straight to north till you have another sign to turn righ. Anoth 3-400m and you are at the Collage.
